Clear Skies Expected This Weekend

December 2025
by: Mount Fuji Watch

Weather reports expect clear skies around Lake Kawaguchi, offering a full view of Mount Fuji. Morning hours between 6:00–8:00 AM usually provide the best visibility.

Bring warm clothing as temperatures drop quickly even when the weather looks sunny. Thermal layers and gloves are recommended for long photo sessions.

Transportation may be crowded on clear days, so arriving early helps avoid long lines around popular viewing spots.
