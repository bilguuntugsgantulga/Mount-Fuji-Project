Hiking & Trails Around Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji has several popular hiking routes with beautiful views of the mountain, forests, shrines, and lakes.

  • Yoshida Trail

    The most commonly used route and offers sunrise views near the summit.

  • Subashiri Trail

    A quieter route that passes through peaceful forest areas before joining the main path.

  • Fujinomiya Trail

    The shortest and steepest route, offering open views and a direct climb.
